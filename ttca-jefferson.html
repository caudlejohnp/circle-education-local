Texas Tech Coding Academy - Jefferson

120 E Austin St. Jefferson, TX 75657

We offer Full Stack Web Development & Cyber Security classes at this location. In our Full Stack Web Development course you will learn the most popular languages for making websites, web apps, games, animations, and mobile applications. In this track you'll learn front-end and back-end web development, some of today's most versatile and sought-after skills. Classes are taught by full-time, experienced web developers. The Cyber Security course is practical and project-based. It prepares students to achieve and excel in employment in the quickly growing field of cybersecurity (methodologies in defensive, offensive, and cloud based computing). Our courses are developed and taught by instructors and industry professionals who understand industry certifications and provide a focus on competent, continued learning and community contribution. At the end of each course throughout the cybersecurity program, you will be prepared to take one of five CompTIA cybersecurity certifications (Network+, Security+, CySA+, PenTest+, and CASP+). If you are interested in attending this location please feel free to apply online as we do not currently see walk-ins.
